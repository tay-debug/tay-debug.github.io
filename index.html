About me

Hi, I'm Tayfun! I appreciate your curiosity about myself and hope I can provide some insight on how I ended up on this website and what you could expect from me.

Originally I come from a medical background with 3 years of (german) paramedic experience in city rescue service. After reevaluating my life plans and assessing my skills in other sectors, I decided to take a risk and change my life. "Something with programming!", I said to myself when searching for universities back in 2019. I decided to put all my chances into "Virtual Realities" at the SRH University Heidelberg and successfully completed my bachelor in 2022.

During my three years of studying with amazing people I learned a lot about coding, project management and myself. I proceeded to create projects in C++ & C#, learned to use Unity and create interesting VR-applications. I gained an understanding of how to handle bigger project groups, organize my workflow & adjust it in line with my colleagues. "I'd rather ask than stay foolish" was my go-to way to handle issues. I learned to analyze problems and choose the best solution for the current situation - that also meant asking my colleagues, lecturers or the vast internet, so I could learn new things.

Coming from a paramedic background I already had a good grasp of how to handle stressful situations and keep a cool head. But working on software projects together with other people did appear to be an entirely new context for myself, which I was quickly able to adapt to and use my skills to perform at the best quality possible.

Now I'm looking for new opportunities in the software development sector. If I sparked some interest in you, I'd be happy to talk to you about job opportunities within the realms of VR, AR & MR. But as an inquisitive person, I'd also be happy to speak about any other new possibilities I could be part of.
You can contact me via e-mail or find me on LinkedIn and Xing.
